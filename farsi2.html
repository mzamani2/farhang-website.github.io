Twelve one-and-a-half hours classes for only €240

This series of lessons are intended for anyone who wants to learn Persian from scratch.


By the end of this course, you learn:

  • How to describe your family, age, classes, & how you get somewhere
  • How to express what you like/dislike, need/have & common Persian courtesy phrases
  • How to describe the place of objects/people
