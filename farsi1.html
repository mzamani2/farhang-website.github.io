This series of lessons are intended for anyone who wants to learn Persian from scratch.

  • 12 classes of 1.5 hours
  • Costs: €240

By the end of this course, you learn:

  • Persian alphabet & sound system
  • Writing & reading single words
  • Basic numbers (1 -- 10)
  • Basic conversational skills (e.g., introducing yourself, your profession, hometown)
